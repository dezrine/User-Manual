Chapter 1

Introduction

About PayFlow

PayFlow is a comprehensive Payroll and Human Resource Management System designed for Jamaican businesses. It handles employee time tracking, payroll calculation with statutory deductions, payslip generation, employee data management, and financial reporting.

Key Features

l Real-Time Time Tracking

Clock in/out with live session timer tracking hours in real-time.

l  Automated Payroll

Calculate payroll instantly with JMD 700/hour rate and automatic deductions.

l Jamaican Tax Compliance

Built-in NIS, NHT, Education Tax, and PAYE calculations.

l Role-Based Security

Three user levels: Employee, Admin, CEO with appropriate access.

l Comprehensive Reporting

Generate salary lists, deduction summaries, and financial statements.

l Registration Key System

Secure Admin/CEO account creation with key-based registration and approval workflow.

l Meeting Scheduling

CEO can schedule staff meetings with audience selection (All or Admins Only).

l Task Management

Modern task system for all users. Admins can assign tasks to employees.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Chapter 2

Getting Started

Creating an Account

Employee Account

1. Open PayFlow in your browser

2. Click "Don't have an account? Sign Up"

3. Enter Username and Password

4. Enter Employee ID (e.g., E001)

5. Select "Employee Account"

6. Click "Create Account"

Admin/CEO Account

1. Get registration key from CEO

2. Follow steps above but select "Admin" or "CEO"

3. Enter the registration key when prompted

4. Click "Create Account"

5. Wait for admin approval before logging in

Important: Admin/CEO accounts require a registration key and admin approval.

Logging In

1. Enter your Username or Name

2. Enter your Password

3. Select your Role from dropdown

4. Click "Sign In"

Note: You can use either your username or your full name to log in.

Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: What is the hourly rate? A: All employees are paid JMD 700 per hour.

Q: How is pay calculated? A: Hours worked × JMD 700, minus statutory deductions (NIS, NHT, Education Tax, PAYE).

Q: When is payday? A: Check your dashboard for the scheduled payday date.

Q: Why are there deductions? A: Jamaican law requires NIS, NHT, Education Tax, and PAYE deductions.

Account & Access

Q: How do I create an Admin account? A: Get a registration key from the CEO, enter it during signup, then wait for admin approval.

Q: Can employees see admin-only announcements? A: No, only "Everyone" announcements are visible to employees. "Admins Only" announcements are restricted.

Q: What if I forget my password? A: Contact your system administrator to reset your password.

Time Tracking

Q: What if I forget to clock out? A: Contact your administrator to adjust the record manually.

Q: Can I clock in early? A: Yes, you can clock in at any time. The system tracks your actual hours.

Task Management FAQ

Q: Who can assign tasks? A: Admins and CEO can assign tasks to employees. All users can create personal tasks.

Q: How do I mark a task as complete? A: Click the "Mark Complete" button on the task card, or go to Task Management and toggle the status.

Q: Can I edit tasks assigned to me? A: You can mark them complete/incomplete, but only the creator or an admin can edit task details.

Q: What are task priorities? A: Low (blue), Medium (yellow), High (orange), Urgent (red).

Meeting & Announcements FAQ

Q: Where do meetings appear? A: Meetings appear in the announcements section and on all relevant user dashboards.

Q: Can I see meetings scheduled for admins only? A: Only if you are an Admin or CEO. Employees only see "General" meetings.

Q: How do I know if a meeting is for me? A: Check the audience badge - "All" means everyone, "Admins" means admins only.

Data & Security FAQ

Q: Can deleted data be restored? A: No, deletions are permanent. Always backup your data before purging records.

Q: How often should I backup? A: Regular backups are recommended, especially before major operations like payroll generation or purging.

Q: Who can see my personal data? A: Employees can see their own data. Admins and CEO can see all employee data for management purposes.

Payroll FAQ

Q: How often is payroll generated? A: Typically monthly, per your company's schedule. Check with your administrator.

Q: What if my hours are incorrect? A: Contact your administrator to review and correct your attendance records.

Q: When can I view my payslip? A: Payslips are available immediately after payroll is generated and saved by the administrator.
